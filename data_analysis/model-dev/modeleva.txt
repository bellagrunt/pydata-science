Regression Plot
* Why use regression plot?
    - it gives us a good estimate of:
    1. relationshop between two varaibles
    2. strength of the correlation
    3. direction of relationship (pos or neg)

- Shows a combo of:
    1. scatterplot : where each point represents a different y
    2. fitted linear regression line (y)

    sns.regplot(x="highway-mpg", y="price", data=df)
