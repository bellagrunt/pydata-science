Types of relationships

Building blocks:
- Building blocks of a relationship are:
1. Entities sets,
2. Relationship sets,
3. Crows foot notations

Defining relationships between entities:
- many authors writing a book ... One author writing multiple books
- Relationship between the book and the author

One-to-One relationship:
- A book is written by one author
Book || Authored by || Author 

One-to-Many relationship:
- A book written by many authors
- Crows foot notation, less-than symbol
- Many-to-one relationship for many authors write a single book
Book || Authored By < Author 

Many-to-Many relationship
- Crows foot notation, less than symbol
- Many books written by many authors 
- Many authors writing many books
Book > Authored by < Author
